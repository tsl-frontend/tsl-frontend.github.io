## Sass

- It's called Sass. Extension is .scss
- SCSS = Sassy CSS.
- Sass a superset of CSS

## Sass features

- Nesting
- Variables
- Importing (@import)
- Color manipulation
- Mixins (@include)
- Math
- Exending rulesets (@extends)
- Invisible comments
