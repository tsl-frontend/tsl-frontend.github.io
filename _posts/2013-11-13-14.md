## Jekyll

### Building your site

- `cd` to directory
- `jekyll build`
- Compiled site will go in the `_site` directory

### Starting Jekyll server

- `cd` to directory
- `jekyll serve --watch`
- Go to http://0.0.0.0:4000

### Converting old site to Jekyll

- `cd` to directory
- `jekyll new --blank --force .`s
